Mission

The internet is no longer just a tool, it is where we work, and connect. However, as our lives move online, so do the risks. Internet Safety Basics was created to bridge the gap between complex technical jargon and everyday online safety. This project focuses on empowering users to understand not just what threats exist, but how they function and how to stop them.

Why This Matters Now

With the rise of AI-driven scams and sophisticated data breaches, traditional security methods are often not enough. Understanding modern "cyber stacks" is the first step toward building a truly secure digital footprint.

The Three Pillars of Digital Security
  • 1. Awareness
    Recognizing the psychological tricks hackers use to manipulate users.
  • 2. Protection
    Implementing technical barriers like encryption and multi-factor authentication.
  • 3. Resilience
    Developing habits that ensure even if one layer of security fails, your entire life isn't compromised.
